Marketing Manager

Hetauda, Nepal Full Time RS.45000 - Rs.60000

Job description

The Marketing Manager is responsible for developing and executing marketing strategies to promote a company's products or services. They oversee the planning, development, and execution of all marketing initiatives and campaigns.

Responsibility

The Marketing Manager should have a strong understanding of marketing principles and techniques, as well as experience in managing and leading marketing campaigns. Strong communication, leadership, and project management skills are also essential for success in this role.

  • Developing marketing plans and budgets
  • Identifying target audiences and developing marketing campaigns to reach them
  • Managing relationships with partners and vendors
  • Overseeing the development of promotional materials, such as brochures and website content
  • Coordinating events and trade shows

Qualifications

The qualifications for a Marketing Manager typically include:

  • A bachelor's degree in marketing, business, or a related field
  • Strong understanding of marketing principles, techniques, and tools
  • Experience with market research and data analysis
  • Strong leadership and project management skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Company Detail

Hetauda Cement Factory is a leading manufacturer of high-quality cement products. The company is located in Hetauda 04, a city known for its rich natural resources and vibrant industrial sector. The factory is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and machinery, allowing us to produce a wide range of cement products with consistent quality and high strength. Our products include Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC), Portland Pozzolana Cement (PPC), and Portland Slag Cement (PSC) which are widely used in construction and infrastructure projects. Hetauda Cement Factory is committed to meeting the needs of its customers by providing high-quality products and excellent customer service. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to ensuring that our products meet the highest industry standards.
